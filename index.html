About UNZA Fundflow

The Online Campus Fundraiser System is designed to revolutionize fundraising at the University of Zambia. We replace informal, fragmented methods with a secure, transparent, and centralized platform.

Centralized Campaigns

Bring all campus fundraising activities, from student projects to club events, under one secure roof.

Secure & Transparent

Ensure every donation and ticket purchase is secure, with real-time tracking and full financial accountability.

Enhanced Participation

Make it easier for students, staff, and alumni to support causes they care about and attend campus events.

How It Works

Whether you're an aspiring organizer or a generous supporter, UNZA Fundflow simplifies the process.

For Organizers

  1. Get Your Organization Profile Set Up: Admins will create and manage your organization's profile, providing you access to create campaigns.
  2. Create & Launch Your Campaign: Easily set up fundraising campaigns or ticketed events with goals, descriptions, and media.
  3. Track Progress in Real-Time: Monitor donations, ticket sales, and overall progress via your personalized dashboard.
  4. Manage Funds & Tickets: Handle withdrawals and validate event tickets with integrated QR-code scanning.

For Supporters

  1. Register Your Account: Sign up quickly to become an individual user and start supporting causes.
  2. Discover Campaigns & Events: Browse a wide range of campus initiatives and exciting events.
  3. Make Secure Contributions: Donate to campaigns or purchase tickets using secure online payment methods.
  4. Receive Instant Notifications: Get real-time updates and digital tickets for every successful transaction.
