Why UNZA Fundraiser?

Traditional fundraising at the University of Zambia has often been fragmented, relying on informal methods like WhatsApp groups and manual tracking. This leads to a lack of transparency, difficulty in tracking donations, and a general erosion of trust.

The Online Campus Fundraiser System is here to change that. We provide a centralized, secure, and transparent digital platform.

  • Secure donations & ticketing
  • Real-time progress tracking
  • Enhanced participation & trust
  • Improved financial accountability

How It Works

1

Register & Create

Sign up as an individual user. Organization profiles are added by admins. Easily create and submit your campaign for verification.

2

Donate & Participate

Browse verified campaigns, make secure donations, or purchase tickets for campus events with ease.

3

Track & Grow

Monitor your campaign's progress in real-time, manage contributions, and receive instant transaction notifications.
